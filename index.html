What devices do you offer?

We offer the Clover Mini and Clover Flex completely free, and the Clover Station Duo for only $999 (typically $2,250-$3,250). All devices come with our transparent interchange plus pricing.

Is there a contract or commitment required?

No contract required! We offer a risk-free trial with no long-term commitment. You can cancel anytime without penalties or fees. Try our service with complete peace of mind.

What are your processing rates?

Our pricing is simple and transparent: Interchange plus $0.03 + 0.02% with a $29.99 monthly service fee. No hidden fees, no surprises - just honest, affordable payment processing.

How quickly can I start accepting payments?

Getting started is fast and easy! Sign up online or call us today. We can ship your device within 24-48 hours and have you accepting payments within 2-3 business days.

What types of payments can I accept?

Accept credit cards, debit cards, contactless payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay), chip cards, and swipe payments. Our Clover devices support all major payment methods your customers prefer.
