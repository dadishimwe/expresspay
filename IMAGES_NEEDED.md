## 🎨 Image Style Guide

### Photography Style
- **Lighting:** Bright, professional, natural light when possible
- **Background:** Clean (white/light) or authentic business environment
- **Angle:** Straight-on or slight angle, avoid extreme angles
- **Focus:** Sharp, clear, high quality
- **Color:** Vibrant but professional, match site's color scheme

### Device Photos Specifically
- **Show screens:** Make sure device screens are visible and lit
- **In context:** When possible, show devices in real business use
- **Multiple angles:** Front, side, in-hand for Flex
- **Scale:** Show size relative to common objects when helpful

### People Photos
- **Expression:** Friendly, confident, professional
- **Attire:** Business casual, appropriate for their industry
- **Background:** Simple, not distracting
- **Diversity:** Show variety of business types and people

## ⚙️ How to Replace Images Once You Have Them

### Option 1: Update HTML (Recommended for local hosting)
1. Create `/images/` folder in your site root
2. Add your images following the folder structure above
3. Find and replace in HTML files:
   ```
   OLD: src="https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/[long-path]/image.jpg"
   NEW: src="images/devices/hero/clover-mini-hero.jpg"
   ```

### Option 2: Use Current CDN Structure
If you're hosting on Webflow or similar:
1. Upload images through their interface
2. Images replace automatically at same URLs

### Option 3: Use Your Own CDN
1. Upload to CloudFlare, AWS S3, or similar
2. Replace URLs with your CDN paths
