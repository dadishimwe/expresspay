## ⚡ Quick Customization Tips

### To Update Images:
Look for `<img src="https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/..."` tags and replace URLs with:
- Clover device photos
- Business/retail environments
- Happy merchant photos

### To Update Videos:
Look for `<video>` tags with `data-video-urls` and replace with:
- Payment processing videos
- Business success stories
- Product demonstrations

### To Change Colors:
Search for color codes in the HTML:
- Primary: #0B2641 (dark blue)
- Accent: #FF4006 (orange)
- Secondary: #A37CFF (purple)

### To Update Logo:
Search for "Express Payments" text logos and replace with:
- Your actual logo image
- Update in navigation and footer
